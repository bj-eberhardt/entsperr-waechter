Privacy Policy - Unlock Capture

Last updated: June 3, 2026

Summary: Unlock Capture processes camera and system events to log unlock attempts and optionally create photos or short videos. The app sends no data to a server. Content is stored locally on the device.

1. Controller

This app is intended for use on your own devices. If you publish it, please provide your contact details here as required by Google Play Console policies.

2. What the app does

Unlock Capture helps trace unknown access attempts on a device. When an unlock attempt is detected, for example through an incorrect security code or, depending on the device, a successful unlock event, the app can depending on settings:

  • log an event in the internal history,
  • and/or automatically create a front camera recording.

Recording runs through a foreground service. Android therefore shows a visible notification.

3. What data is processed

3.1 Camera recordings (optional)

  • Photos/videos from the front camera triggered by specific events.
  • Stored locally on the device, for example in Pictures/UnlockCapture and Movies/UnlockCapture.

3.2 Event log (history/audit log)

  • Time and type of an unlock event, depending on app settings and device support.
  • This log is stored locally in the app and can be cleaned up automatically.

3.3 Integrity data (hash)

For recordings, the app may store a cryptographic hash (SHA-256) to detect later whether a file is missing. The hash is stored locally.

4. Data sharing

No sharing. Unlock Capture does not transmit data to third parties and does not use cloud services. There is no login, no account, and no advertising.

5. Permissions

  • Camera: to create photos and videos.
  • Notifications: required for the visible foreground-service notification.
  • Media access (read permissions): to display previously stored recordings in the app.
  • Device admin (if enabled): to detect certain unlock-related events.
  • Biometrics/device credential (optional): to protect app access using fingerprint, face unlock, or PIN.

6. Retention and deletion

  • Recordings remain on the device until they are deleted by the device owner.
  • Logs and integrity data can be automatically limited or cleaned up inside the app, depending on app version and settings.

7. Security

  • Local storage on the device.
  • Optional app access protection via biometrics or PIN.
  • Integrity checks using SHA-256 hashes to detect missing files.

8. Notes on lawful use

Please use the app only on your own devices and only with the device owner’s consent. Follow applicable law, especially privacy and personality rights.
